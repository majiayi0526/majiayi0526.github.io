Research in Progress 在研课题

"A Study on Injury Risk Assessment in Youth Sabre Fencing Based on Computer Vision and Large Language Models" 《基于计算机视觉与大语言模型的青少年佩剑损伤风险评估研究》
M.S. Thesis · Beijing Sport University · 2024 – 硕士学位论文 · 北京体育大学 · 2024年 –
Approach研究思路
A five-stage pipeline — skeleton extraction (YOLOv8-Pose) → 19-indicator biomechanics system → knowledge injection → preference alignment → automated risk assessment — producing interpretable injury-risk reports for youth sabre fencers aged 12–18. 提出五阶段技术管线：骨架提取（YOLOv8-Pose）→ 19 项指标生物力学体系 → 知识注入 → 偏好对齐 → 自动化风险评估，为 12–18 岁青少年佩剑运动员生成 可解释的损伤风险报告。
Novelty创新点
The first application of SFT + DPO two-stage alignment (Qwen2.5-7B + LoRA, LlamaFactory) to sports injury-risk assessment; validated against national-level coach evaluations. 首次将 SFT + DPO 两阶段对齐方法（Qwen2.5-7B + LoRA，LlamaFactory） 应用于运动损伤风险评估，并以国家级教练员评估作为验证基准。

Expert Chain-of-Thought Simulation Applied to Épée Training 专家思维链仿真在重剑训练中的应用
Beijing Sport University Fencing Team 北京体育大学击剑队
Amateur épée athletes commonly face delayed movement feedback, limited interpretation of training data, and difficulty in identifying injury risk in advance. This project uses an executable expert chain-of-thought (CoT) simulation to reproduce the diagnostic pathway of senior coaches. 业余重剑选手训练中常见动作反馈滞后、数据解释不足、损伤风险难以前置识别等问题。 本项目用可执行的专家思维链仿真复现资深教练诊断路径。
